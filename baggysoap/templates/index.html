About

Baggy Soap is a product borne out of a love for the environment and a desire to rid the planet of plastic waste. There is so much focus on carrier bags, disposable cups and straws, yet so many of us generate plastic waste every day in the bathroom, through using shower gel, hand wash and other products in plastic bottles. We want to change that.

We have developed Baggy Soap to offer a superior cleaning experience, leaving both your body and your conscience squeaky clean. Our cotton bags are 100% biodegradable and our handmade soaps have been carefully chosen to work perfectly with our bags. We are committed to using no plastic and welcome feedback or suggestions from our customers.

Contact Us

We are based in south-east London, but we don't have a sales outlet open to the public. If you would like to get in touch with us directly, please contact us at enquiries@baggysoap.co.uk
